The XYZ 1000

  • Engine displacement: 998 cc
  • Max power: 197 hp @ 13,500 rpm
  • Max torque: 83 lb-ft @ 11,500 rpm
  • Top speed: 186 mph
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): 2.6 seconds
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual
  • Brakes: Dual 320 mm discs with Brembo monobloc calipers (front), Single 220 mm disc with Brembo caliper (rear)
  • Suspension: Fully adjustable Öhlins NIX30 fork (front), Fully adjustable Öhlins TTX36 shock (rear)
  • Tires: Pirelli Diablo Supercorsa SP (front and rear)
  • Seat height: 32.7 inches
  • Weight: 441 lbs (dry)

The XYZ 1000 is a high-performance sport motorcycle designed for experienced riders. It is powered by a liquid-cooled, 998 cc engine that produces 197 horsepower and 83 lb-ft of torque, making it capable of reaching speeds up to 186 mph.

The XYZ 1000 features a 6-speed manual transmission and advanced braking system, with dual 320 mm discs and Brembo monobloc calipers in the front and a single 220 mm disc with a Brembo caliper in the rear. The suspension is fully adjustable with an Öhlins NIX30 fork in the front and an Öhlins TTX36 shock in the rear.

This motorcycle rides on Pirelli Diablo Supercorsa SP tires, which provide excellent grip and handling on both the street and the track. The seat height is 32.7 inches, and the weight is 441 lbs (dry). Overall, the XYZ 1000 is a powerful and agile motorcycle that is perfect for riders who demand the highest level of performance.
